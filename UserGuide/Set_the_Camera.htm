Set the Camera

 

 

Add a floor plane by clicking the Plane object type and dragging out underneath the car.

For a ray-tracing renderer like AMD FireRender it is recommended that we use a Physical camera.  

Create a physical camera and place it where you want it by selecting the Physical button underneath the Camera tab.  Click on the viewport to place the camera then drag to pull out the camera's field of view.  Right click on the camera and select Move  so you can left it up above the car.  Rotate the field of vie to frame the car

Right click on the camera and select Move  so you can left it up above the car.  Rotate the field of view to frame the car.  If the camera is Targeted this will happen automatically.

Right click the [Perspective] in the top left hand corner of the view and select Camera -> PhysCamera001 to view through the camera you have just placed.

If you want you can do a test render.  You will see that the renderer has set the view to PhysCamera001.  Right click the [PhysCamera001] and change it back to Perspective or push P.
