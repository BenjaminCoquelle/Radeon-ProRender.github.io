Add Additional Lights

Add a Photometric light to the scene, select the target light and place it over the car, Change the lights intensity parameter to 45000 (cd).

In the Render Setup under the Common -> Render Output click the Files and select a directory where you want to save the rendered output.

Through your Physical camera take another Render. You will get a option to replace the previous render. For this tutorial we will be replacing it.

Rendered Image

 

Lets change the color of the body.

Open the material editor.  Find the Car_Paint_Blue material that we used for the car body.  If you can't find it you can click on the Select Material from Object button in the menu of the material editor.

 

You could always just apply another material from the fire render material library.  But for this we will just be changing this materials color.

Double click the FireRender Color node, change the color to a red.

 

Take another render.  This time the car should be Red.  All the FireRender materials are customizable.
